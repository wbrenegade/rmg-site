RMG Decal Preview Tool

Upload a car photo, upload a decal, place it on the vehicle, blend it, and export the preview.

1. Upload Images

Transparent PNG or SVG decals work best. Flat black cut-file style graphics preview the cleanest.

2. Placement Preset

This gives a starting position. Drag the green corner handles to fit the car angle and panel shape.

3. Transform

4. Realism / Blend

Curve warp bends the decal slightly before placing it. This helps fake body curvature on doors, fenders, and quarter panels.

5. Color Options

Works best on black/white decals. Good for showing matte black, gloss black, copper, red, etc.
